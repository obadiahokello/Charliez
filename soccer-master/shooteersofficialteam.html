2024 CHARLIE'S SHOOTERS SQUAD

CHARLIE'S SHOOTERS ANNOUNCE 2024S SQUAD

Get ready MACOS League fans, because Charlie's Shooters are back and unveiling a lineup that promises to shake up the competition. With a mix of returning veterans and exciting new talent, the 2024 squad is poised to build upon the team's championship legacy.

Goalkeepers: The Wall Between the Posts

  • Okello Obadiah: A shot-stopping force with lightning-quick reflexes.
  • Mubiru Isaac: Known for his commanding presence and distribution skills.
  • Opio Edwin Benjamin: A rising star with a bright future in goal.

Defenders: The Unbreakable Backbone

  • Mubiru Ricardo: A tenacious defender who reads the game brilliantly.
  • Wadidi: A powerhouse in the backline, known for dominant aerial play.
  • Odoch: Technical and quick, his ability to shut down opposing wingers is unmatched.
  • Akemkene: A versatile defender with strength and tackling prowess.
  • Ekaa Mathew: Provides depth and reliability to the defensive unit.

Midfielders: The Creative Engine

  • Edwin: A playmaker with exceptional vision and passing range.
  • Male John: Tenacious and skillful, he drives attacks from the midfield
  • Mandu John: A goalscoring threat with clinical finishing ability.
  • Musoke Andrew: The midfield anchor, winning tackles and shielding the defense.
  • Muduku: A dynamic presence with a flair for the unexpected.
  • Ayuma Joseph: Adds energy and creativity to the midfield battle.
  • Spectre Cuzo: The maestro, his pinpoint passes unlock defenses.

Forwards: The Finishing Touch

  • Ssempeke: A pacey striker who stretches defenses and creates havoc.
  • Kadala Hamza: A clinical finisher with a knack for being in the right place.
  • Alinda: A versatile attacker offering speed and skill on the flanks.

This carefully assembled squad boasts a blend of experience, raw talent, and a hunger to win. Under the guidance of Coach Timothy Ainebyona, Charlie's Shooters are aiming to not only defend their title but to dominate the MACOS League.

Will the competition be able to withstand the firepower of this 2024 team? Only time will tell, but this season promises to be one that MACOS League fans won't soon forget.
